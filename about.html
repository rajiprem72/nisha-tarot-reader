About the Reader

My Journey Into Tarot

My journey with tarot began as a deeply personal and spiritual calling. What started as a path of self-discovery gradually transformed into years of guiding and helping people through life’s emotional, financial, spiritual, and practical challenges.

Through many of my readings, I realized that tarot is not just a tool for guidance — it is also a powerful medium for healing that helps provide solutions, creates self-awareness, and supports transformation.

Over time, many clients and seekers began expressing a desire to learn tarot themselves — not just to predict outcomes, but to understand energy, intuition, emotional patterns, and spiritual growth.

This inspired me to expand my work from personal tarot readings into conducting online tarot classes and mentorship programs.

Reading Style

My online classes are designed to make tarot simple, practical, intuitive, and spiritually meaningful for beginners as well as advanced learners.

I focus not only on card meanings, but also on developing intuition, understanding energy connections, life patterns, ethical reading practices, confidence building, and real-life application of tarot guidance.

Life Challenges

Life brings many challenges — emotional confusion, relationship struggles, career uncertainty, financial stress, health concerns, self-doubt, and feelings of being misunderstood and disconnected.

Often, people feel lost because they cannot clearly understand why certain patterns keep repeating in their lives.

Through tarot, we help clients gain clarity about these hidden emotional blocks. Tarot acts as a mirror to the subconscious mind, revealing the energies, fears, karmic lessons, and opportunities influencing a person’s life journey.

Whether someone is facing heartbreak, career delays, financial instability, family stress, emotional imbalance, or confusion about their next step, my teachings are designed to provide insight, direction, and emotional support.

This enables practical clarity to help clients make more aligned decisions with confidence.
