Other Divination Modalities

ᚱ

Runes Divination

Runes divination is a spiritual and symbolic practice that uses ancient alphabet symbols called “runes” for guidance, reflection, and intuitive insight. It is deeply connected with old Norse and Germanic traditions from Northern Europe.

🕯️

Candle Wax Reading

Candle wax reading, also known as ceromancy, is a form of divination where the shapes, patterns, and residue left behind after a candle burns are interpreted for spiritual symbolism, reflection, and guidance.

🌟

Life Coach Mastery

Advanced learning and personal development programs focused on becoming a skilled life coach. A life coach helps individuals improve areas such as:

  • Goals and Motivation
  • Confidence Building
  • Career Direction
  • Relationships
  • Productivity
  • Mindset and Habits
💫

EFT / TFT

EFT (Emotional Freedom Techniques) and TFT (Thought Field Therapy) are emotional wellness, stress-management, and self-help tapping techniques.

Classes Include:

  • Learning tapping points
  • Guided tapping exercises
  • Discussion about emotions and mindset
🔮

Crystal Healing Courses

Learn how crystals are used in spiritual and holistic wellness practices. Courses focus on energy work, meditation, chakra concepts, relaxation techniques, and symbolic meanings associated with different crystals.

Courses Designed For:

  • Personal Spiritual Learning
  • Self-Reflection and Mindfulness
  • Holistic Wellness Practices
  • Becoming a Wellness or Spiritual Practitioner
