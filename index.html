About Tarot Reading

Tarot reading is a powerful intuitive tool that helps you understand life situations, emotions, and future possibilities. It is not about fixed predictions, but about guidance and awareness.

History

Originating in Europe during the 14th century, tarot cards were initially used for games. Over time, they evolved into a spiritual system connected with symbolism, astrology, and human psychology.

Significance

Tarot helps individuals gain clarity, improve decision-making, and understand hidden influences in relationships, career, and life.

Reliability

Tarot reflects current energies and possibilities. It depends on the reader’s intuition and the seeker’s openness. It is a guidance system, not a fixed prediction.

Cost

Tarot readings typically range from ₹500 to ₹5000 depending on duration and depth of consultation.

Remedies (Parigarams)

Based on readings, simple remedies such as meditation, affirmations, better decision-making, and energy alignment practices may be suggested.
