Short Introduction

With over 10 years of experience as a tarot coach, healer, life coach, and NLP practitioner, my teaching approach combines life solutions with practical understanding. I believe tarot should empower people, help them trust their inner wisdom, and create inner and outer growth for themselves as well as others.

Today, my mission is not only to guide people through readings, but also to help others become confident tarot readers who can use this sacred practice for self-growth, healing, passive income, and helping others.

Through online learning, I am able to connect with students across different places and create a supportive community built on trust, clarity, and transformation.
